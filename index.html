Top three activities to do in Burton

Visit St. Modwen's Church

St. Modwen's Church is a remarkable piece of Burton's history with its stunning architecture. It's an inspiring spot for anyone interested in historical buildings and local heritage.

Enjoy the Peace of the Trent & Mersey Canal

The Trent & Mersey Canal is a great place for a peaceful walk or a relaxing bike ride. Its natural beauty and calm surroundings make it a great escape from the daily grind.

Experience the Charm of Tutbury Castle

Tutbury Castle offers a unique journey into medieval times. Its well-preserved ruins and panoramic views are not only impressive but also tell stories of a bygone era.
